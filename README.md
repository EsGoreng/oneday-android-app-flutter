# 🌟 OneDay: Your Journey to a Better You  

**Why wait for 'someday' when your journey to a better you starts with OneDay?**  

OneDay is a revolutionary app that integrates all the tools you need to achieve **financial wellness, daily productivity, and mental well-being**.  
No more switching between multiple apps—everything you need is now at your fingertips, beautifully designed in one **personalized dashboard**.  

---

## ✨ About This Project
OneDay is designed to help you **take full control of your life**.  
We combine three essential pillars—**finance, habits, and mood**—into a seamless platform.  

Start your journey to becoming more **organized, productive, and happier** with OneDay.  

---

## 🚀 Key Features  

### 💸 Smarter Financial Management (FINANCE)  
- 📝 **Transaction Tracker** – Easily record income and expenses.  
- 💰 **Smart Budgeting** – Create monthly or recurring budgets.  
- 🏦 **Saving Goals** – Set goals and watch your savings grow.  
- 📊 **Data Visualization** – Intuitive charts for income & spending.  
- 🛠️ **Extra Tools** – Built-in calculator & private financial notes.  

### 🎯 Build Better Habits (HABIT)  
- 🗓️ **Productivity Calendar** – Schedule tasks & recurring routines.  
- 🔔 **Smart Reminders** – Unique notifications to keep you on track.  
- ⏱️ **Custom Timers** – Presets for focus, reading, or workouts.  
- 🔥 **Progress Heatmap** – Visualize consistency in habit building.  
- 📓 **Habit Notes** – Record your thoughts & track progress.  

### 😊 Understand Your Mood (MOOD)  
- 😄 **Daily Mood Tracker** – Log your mood with expressive visuals.  
- 📅 **Mood Calendar** – Review your emotional patterns over time.  
- ✍️ **Feelings Journal** – Reflect on your thoughts and emotions.  
- 📈 **Mood Statistics** – Summarized insights for deeper self-awareness.  

---

## 🌟 Personalized Experience
- 🏠 **Main Dashboard** – Daily summary of finance, habits, and mood, plus motivational quotes & fun facts.  
- 👤 **Avatar Customization** – Create a fun, unique avatar that represents you.
